You will from time to time need to setup, manage, or change your account settings.

From your account page, you can manage:

  • Account Settings - such as username, password, and billing email
  • Additional Users - add or remove users from your SendGrid account
  • Alert Settings - add, remove, or modify SendGrid email alerts about your account
  • Billing History - view previous invoices and amounts
  • Billing Package Changes - upgrade or downgrade your account
  • Billing Settings - add or change your credit card for automatic payments
  • Global Settings - convert plain text emails to HTML automatically
  • Profile Settings - add or update information about yourself, such as website, phone number, and timezone
  • Two Factor Authentication - ask SendGrid to require something only you would have in order to log in
