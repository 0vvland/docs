Undelivered email can be the result of several things but most commonly there is an issue with the email address or content. Once an email has been attempted and has been classified as bounced, invalid or a spam report subsequent sends to the same email address will be considered a drop. https://sendgrid.com/docs/User_Guide/Email_Deliverability/Undelivered_Email/drops.html https://sendgrid.com/docs/User_Guide/Email_Deliverability/Undelivered_Email/spam.html
