Configuring the EventKit App

The EventKit is an open source app that our Support Incubator team built. This app allows you to easily setup our Event API on your server and provides a easy to use reporting system. Ron goes into detail on how to setup the EventKit on your server and demonstrates its basic and useful functionality. Please keep in mind that this is open source and we do not provide support for this app.
