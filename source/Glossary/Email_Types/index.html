--- layout: page weight: 0 title: Types of Emails navigation: show: true seo: title: Types of Emails override: true description: There are really two types of emails, Transactional and Marketing. These examples all fall under one or the other. --- If your company is sending emails, you have likely seen or heard of one of these types of email campaigns. Marketing Email Types * Newsletters * Peer Initiated Invitations - A campaign where customers can invite their friends and family to sign up for your newsletter, products, and services. Be careful, there are right and wrong ways to do this! * Product Announcements * Promotional Emails * Reconfirmation Email Campaign - Ask your customers to confirm again that they want your marketing information. This campaign type is used to re-engage your users and clean house of unengaged customers. Transactional Email Types * Email account ownership confirmations * Shipping Confirmations * Password Reset notifications * Purchase Receipts * System Notifications
